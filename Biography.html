BIOGRAPHY

Julian Smith-McLallen is a keyboardist and producer. Though his roots are in classical and jazz, he has gained experience playing in a wide variety of groups, styles, and venues. Though live shows are Julian’s strong point, his passion lies in the creative atmosphere of the studio, home or professional. Where he likes to record, produce, and compose. Julian began taking classical piano lessons at the age of 6, but soon switched to jazz. Since then, he has continued to branch out into other styles of music, chief among them being brazilian, funk/fusion, salsa, hip-hop and rock. Alongside Julian’s instrumental journey, he has also continued to explore music production, taking inspiration from a wide range of influences. Today, Julian is studying at Berklee College, pursuing a bachelor’s in professional music and a minor in music business, diversifying his skill set. He currently works professionally under the Xhoja Music Agency and also with up and coming artists in the school, mainly on keys and assisting in production.
